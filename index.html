Who Was America's First Treasury Secretary?

Perhaps, you first heard about Alexander Hamilton in the infamous 1993 "Got Milk?" commerical. Or maybe, you know him from staring down at a ten dollar bill. Or you're an obsessive fan of Lin-Manuel Miranda's 11-time Tony Award-winning musical. Regardless of how you came to learn of his existence, he is an important historical figure for good reason. Throughout his lifetime, Alexander Hamilton was many things: a survivor, immigrant, writer, lawyer, war veteran, politician, and Founding Father. With this site, we hope to shine a light on this incredible human being.
